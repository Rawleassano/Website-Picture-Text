Sugar Apple also known as custard apple or sweetsops, is a edible round, ovoid, or cone shaped fruit that is grown widely in tropical areas. Trees rarely exceed 15 to 20 ft (4.6-6.1 m) in height and spread. The skin colour of the sugar-apple fruit changes from bright green to a pale whitish-green as the fruit ripens while having a white or very pale yellow sweet flesh. Sugar-apple is high in energy, an excellent source of vitamin C and manganese, a good source of thiamine and vitamin B6, and provides Vitamins like Riboflavin (B2), Niacin (B3), Pantothenic acid (B5), Folate (B9), iron, magnesium, phosphorus and potassium in fair quantities.

The Star Apple fruit having numerous names is large and rounded from about 6 - 7 centimetres in diameter, tree grows about 7.5–15 m (25–50 feet) tall. This fruit skin is shiny, smooth and purpleish or light green fading from layers into the flesh which contains small amounts of milky juice, somewhat fibrous, sweet, mild and pleasant tasting and vary in colours sometimes white or purple. When cut in cross-section reveals a star-shaped pattern formed by the carpels. Star apples serve as a good source of calcium and adequate magnesium, vitamin C, vitamin B, Minerals, Phosphorus, Amino acids-Tryptophan, -lysine -Methionine etc

Jackfruit is a large, prickly fruit that grows on a tropical tree that can reach an estimated 80 foot tall. Although being a fruit it is sometimes cooked and used or refered to as vegetable ,flesh inside a jackfruit is unique but have a similiar tast to pineapple, mango, banana and not the easiest fruit to prepare. It has a light pale green on the outside and when ripe becomes yellowish greenish looking prickly skin When cut open, you'll see large, pale-yellow pods (or bulbs) that contain seeds, all of which are connected to the fruit's core. After ripening, they turn brown and deteriorate rather quickly. Jackfruits have good source of Vitamin A, Vitamin C Riboflavin, Magnesium, Potassium, Copper, Manganese , etc.

Rambutan fruit is oval and has a bright redcoulour the size of an hen eggs covers with long soft spines and has a tasty acid pulp,tree of the rambutan fruit can reach up to 80 feet (27 meters) in height and thrives best in tropical climates.. Once you peel the skin, the fruit inside looks like a smooth and moist white ball. For lack of a better comparison, it looks a little bit like a cross between a pickled onion and an egg.Inside the fruity flesh there is a large seed that is slightly toxic to humans in its raw state, so don’t eat it.Vitamins found within this fruit can be Vitamin C Manganese, Copper, Potassium, Magnesium, Vitamin B2, Vitamin B3, Iron ,Phosphorus ,Calcium, Vitamin B1, Zinc ,etc.

The Soursop fruit tree is low-branching and reaches a height of 25 or 30 ft (7.5-9 m). The fruit is more or less oval or heart-shaped, some times irregular, lopsided or curved, The skin is dark-green becoming slightly yellowish-green when mature which is soft to the touch. The flesh of the fruit is snow-white, fibrous having juicy segments. Soursop consist of Vitamin C, Potassium, Magnesium, Thiamine, Fiber , Calcium, Phosphorus, Iron , Ascorbic Acid, Amino Acids and others.
