Fruits are delicious, make colourful and flavourful compliments to dishes.They can be found within different times and temperatures of the world ranging in many shapes and sizes and are loved for their nutritious and beneficial intake. Some fruits can carry high or low percentages of nutrients maybe edible or inedible and may numerous to none benefits towards the life of animals.

Some fruits rare to a point where they can only be found in minimum of ten to one region of the world. These are called exotic fruits, their grown only in a specific location or area and carries numerous benefits that are being used in the aid of a healthy lifestyle.
